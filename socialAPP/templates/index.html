Mentions

Through certain keywords that the user enters into the search engine, they can see comments that are important to them from various social networks like Twitter, Facebook, and Instagram. We aim for them to be able to view each of these and access them.

Statistics of mentions

You will be able to directly see the following statistics for each mention: Spoken frequency of the topic, temporal trends, and platform where the highest number of mentions about the topic are seen.

Detailed mention

In the mention or post that interests you the most, you will be able to access it to see the following: The social network where it was published, date and time, all the content of it, and finally, who published it.

Actualized keywords

You will be able to save multiple keywords that lead them to the most important information from each of the mentioned applications, thus enabling them to view the desired information.
